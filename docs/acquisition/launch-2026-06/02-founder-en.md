## Longer version

I’m building Embir, a dating experience organized around declared intent and reciprocal relevance.

The current version focuses on:

- intent-first discovery;
- explicit preferences;
- a mobile-first landing experience;
- anonymous previews before account creation when real public profiles are available;
- a careful city-by-city launch instead of a huge programmatic surface.

The product is still young, so I’m not trying to make it look bigger than it is. The goal is to make the promise clear, the onboarding more respectful, and the public pages honest enough to trust.
