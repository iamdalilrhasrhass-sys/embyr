## Maker comment

Hey Product Hunt,

I’m Dalil, building Embir.

Most dating apps start with the feed. Embir starts one step earlier: what are you actually looking for, and who is mutually relevant to that intention?

The current launch focuses on:

- intent-first onboarding;
- reciprocal preference logic;
- mobile-first storytelling;
- a “discover before signing up” flow;
- anonymous previews when real public profiles are available;
- a careful city-by-city growth strategy.

This is still early. I’m intentionally avoiding inflated community claims and treating the launch as a feedback loop before scaling.

I’d love feedback on the clarity of the landing page, the trust model, and whether the discovery flow makes signup feel more respectful.
