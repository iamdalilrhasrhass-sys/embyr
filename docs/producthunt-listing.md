# EMBIR — Product Hunt Listing

## Tagline
Free, inclusive dating platform. No paywalls, no bots, just real connections.

## Description
**What is EMBIR?**
EMBIR is a dating platform built from the ground up to be free and inclusive. No subscriptions, no "premium" tiers, no algorithmic manipulation. We believe dating apps should connect people, not extract money.

**Why we built it**
We got tired of dating apps that feel like slot machines — designed to keep you swiping and paying, not meeting people. EMBIR is different: it's free forever, and it works for everyone regardless of orientation.

**Key features:**
- 🔓 **100% free** — no paywalls, no premium features hidden behind subscriptions
- 🌈 **Truly inclusive** — set your orientation and gender identity, see only compatible profiles
- 🤖 **No bots** — real verification keeps fake profiles out
- 🔒 **Privacy-first** — your data isn't sold to advertisers
- 📍 **Paris launch** — starting with one city to get the experience right before expanding

**Who's it for?**
Everyone. Gay, straight, bi, trans, queer — you choose who you want to see and who sees you. No algorithmic assumptions about who you should date.

**What's next?**
We're a small, bootstrapped team. Right now EMBIR is live in Paris with ~200 early users. We're iterating fast based on real feedback. Next: expanding to more cities.

**Try it:** [embir.xyz/paris](https://embir.xyz/paris)
